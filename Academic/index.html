MCM: The Mathematical Contest in Modeling

I participated this modeling contest with two of my friends, Shiqing Yu, and Tianwen Ma. Shiqing Yu is the leader of our team.

In order to evaluate the performances of college coaches hired during the past century, we applied R to analyze the college sports data, implemented linear regression to compute the distribution variances of data, and then used AHP (Analytic hierarchy process) to measure each factor.
